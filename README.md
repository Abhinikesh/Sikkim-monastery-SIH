## 📅 Cultural Calendar Highlights

| Festival / Ritual | When | Significance |
|-------------------|------|--------------|
| 🎉 Losar Festival | March | Tibetan New Year with traditional dances & butter sculptures |
| 🪔 Buddha Purnima | May | Birth of Buddha — prayers at all major monasteries |
| 🙏 Durga Puja | September | Victory of good over evil, Puja ceremonies |
| 🍵 Cherry Tea Festival | November | Local cherry & teal culture, fairs & tourism |
| 🎊 Losoong / Namsoong | December | Sikkimese New Year harvest festival |
| 🙏 Prayer Wheel Ritual | Weekly | Spinning prayer wheels, mantras for spiritual purification |
| 🌕 Full Moon Ceremony | Monthly | Extended meditation & community prayers |

## ✈️ Travel Tips

| Topic | Info |
|-------|------|
| **Entry Requirements** | Indian nationals — no permit. Foreign nationals — Inner Line Permit (ILP) required |
| **Best Time to Visit** | March–June and September–November |
| **What to Wear** | Modest clothing — cover shoulders & knees, remove shoes in prayer halls |
| **Photography** | Always ask permission inside monasteries |
| **Transportation** | Local taxis or shared jeeps; roads are steep and winding |
| **Health & Safety** | Acclimatize gradually, stay hydrated, carry basic medications |
